<p><strong>Mattie Sharp was never special—except when it came to getting in trouble. <em>Then,</em> she was exceptional.</strong></p>
<p>So when she falls into a portal and winds up in dragon-filled Cavernis, imagine her surprise when she discovers she’s the heir to a magical prophesy and must train to be the hero she never thought she could be.</p>
<p>Though Mattie has always been a top student, studying at a school for dragons puts even her to the test!</p>
<p>Join Mattie and her crew of new friends as they learn not just how to survive in the world of dragons, but to save it as well.</p>
